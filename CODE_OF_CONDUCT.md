# Code of conduct

We want Deep Work to be a welcoming, professional, and technically rigorous open
source community. Be respectful, assume good intent while discussing evidence,
criticize ideas rather than people, protect private information, and make space for
new contributors.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate exposure of secrets or personal
data, and sustained disruptive behavior are not acceptable. Maintainers may edit,
hide, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate these rules.

Before public community launch, maintainers must publish a private reporting
contact and an enforcement/escalation policy. Until then, do not include sensitive
conduct reports in public issues.
